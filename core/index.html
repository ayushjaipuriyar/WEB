The Novotrix. Ultimatrix levelled up.

The universe was on the verge of chaos.The power of the Ultimatrix was depleted and Ben Tennyson was exhausted. It was up to Azmuth to restore the balance of the universe. The most advanced omnitrix was made out of Azmuth's wrath combined with the universe's wail for help. The desperate conditions gave rise to the most powerful peacekeeping force the universe had ever seen. The NOVOTRIX.

The NOVOTRIX includes the DNA samples of more than ten million alien species, capable of loading a thousand and five DNA samples at any given time, donning extreme abilities, so much so that the fate of the whole universe resides in the wrist of the wearer.
